Dictionary of Terms

Your one stop shop for that one French term you don't have any clue about.


A general dictionary of cooking terms. If something is missing send us a question and we will be sure to keep this page updated!

Boil

To cook in boiling liquid; a liquid is at a boil when there are many bubble rising vigorously to the surface.

Chop

To cut food into small pieces with a knife or other cutting tool.

Dice

To cut food into small cubes of uniform size and shape. Usually these pieces should be around 1/4 inch in size.
