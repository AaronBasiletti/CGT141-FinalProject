Ramen Sauce

Ingredients

  • • Ramen Noodles
  • • Soy Sauce
  • • Honey
  • • Red pepper flakes
  • • Minced garlic / garlic paste / garlic powder
  • • Onion powder
  • • Hot sauce of choice
  • • Corn starch

Directions

  1. Cook noodles until almost done. Drain water.

  2. In small saucepan add all ingredients. Allow to simmer on low for 5 minutes or until it thickens.

  3. Add noodles to saucepan, coating in sauce, allow to simmer ~1minute to finish cooking noodles.

  4. Devour.
