Chicken and Chickpea Tikka Masala

Ingredients
  • • 1 jar of store bought tikka masala simmer sauce of choice
  • • 1 lb chicken
  • • 1 can chickpeas
  • • 1 white or yellow onion
  • • Minced garlic
  • • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Add oil to pan, heat for ~1 min. White oil is heating chop onion and chicken.

  2. Add onion, chicken, and minced garlic to pan. Cook on medium until chicken is cooked through.

  3. Add chickpeas and tikka masala simmer sauce, stir. Simmer on med for 5-10 minutes.

  4. Devour.
